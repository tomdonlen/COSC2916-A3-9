Porject Scope & Limits

Having a realistic and achievable scope is imperative to an efficient workflow, and S.K.I.M is no exception. In terms of hardware and design, purchasing, developing and building a functioning prototype is beyond our time constrains. Instead we will aim to have the product represented in a 3D model with technical drawings outlining each of the hardware components that will make up the device. On the software side, training a machine learning AI to identify different products from images will be priority over having an interactive interface for an end user. This will also aid in the development of databases and file structures. Limiting ourselves from scope creep and wanting more and more features will allow us to achieve our original set out goals.
