Group Processes

Through communication and teamwork
much can be developed.

When our group first met to start discussing this assignment, we started implementing processes we had used in the past assignment such as splitting the sections up into tasks, allocating tasks evenly across the members with attention to areas of expertise for each person. A new process that was implemented for assignment three was assigning a lead and a support to each artefact that was to be developed. This served as another form of proof reading and peer review, it was also used as a method of having multiple people looking into the same artefact if multiple members allocated themselves onto the same task.

Since the assignment was centered around the project idea from assignment two, Nathan took lead on the creative and logistical direction for the product. Throughout our initial meetings we extended on the original ideas presented in assignment two by Nathan, this helped in the allocation of tasks too. Our group has approached both assignment two and three with an aim to complete the content needed for the final report document first. Getting this finalized first has allowed us to create a smooth process of moving all written work into one cohesive document, and then adapting it to a website format.
