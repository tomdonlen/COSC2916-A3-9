Future Testing

Testing the product ourselves will be a vital part of the project success as we must have clear images of local products that can be sent to TensorFlow. The original dataset of training images will come from the testing of the product in development. Testing would begin with confirming the viability of the individual streams before the working prototype is created. The improved iterations of the device would be subsequently tested by quality analysts, to insure we have designed the product with sufficient robustness to interact with the end-user, in ways we had not anticipated. We could complete the testing in-house or source the testing to an external vendor, coordinated by our stream leads and with oversight by Head of UX, depending on capacity within the team. The number and diversity of testers needed will grow as the demand for further, and more specific, testing grows in step with the complexity of product. It will enter different stages of testing internally and externally.
