Ideal Roles

Role 1 - Full Stack Engineer

Key Requirements

  • Bachelor degree or greater in Software Engineering, IT or similar
  • Significant experience in SQLite, JS, Python, MongoDB, Git
  • Cloud Certifications, strong UX/UI principles
  • Strong server-side experience
  • Ability to produce well-structured HTML utilising efficient CSS

Great problem solver, ability to work with other team members in a respectable and efficient manner. Experience in start-up environments and desire to improve and expand on existing work.

Role 2 – Small Systems Engineer

Key Requirements

  • Bachelors degree of greater in IT or similar and/or related field experience with certifications
  • Experience with Raspberry Pi models, particularly Raspberry Pi 4 Model B
  • Knowledge of compatible parts and peripherals, general hardware knowledge
  • Experience in implementing software, particularly machine learning and network communication. Ability to update new and existing microsystems software
  • Ability to configure wireless communications with other devices

Good communication skills and a friendly manner to collaborate with other team members is necessary. Open to expressing new and unique ideas to help further develop through new or original technology.

Role 3 – Machine Learning Engineer

Key Requirements

  • Bachelors degree or greater in IT, Computer Science or similar
  • Proficiency with using Tensorflow, Lobe or similar
  • Experience in developing and training models, deploying and maintaining such models as well
  • Familiarity with databases (SQLite) and Git
  • Understanding of cloud services

Good communication and teamwork skills necessary. Ability to adapt and overcome potential challenges and good problem solving skills required.

Role 4 – Sales and Marketing Manager

Key Requirements

  • Bachelors degree in Marketing, Advertising or Business required
  • Previous experience in a start-up necessary
  • Great communication skills and customer facing experience
  • Developed knowledge of social media advertising and influencing
  • Some knowledge of IT preferred, ability to convey information to potential clients

A self-driven, confident and an ambitious attitude will go far. Time-management and self-discipline will be required. Openness to learn new things, and professional demeanour are nice to haves.
