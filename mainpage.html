The Introduction

So what is S.K.I.M?  

Our Smart Kitchen Inventory Manager, or  S.K.I.M, is a smart device that keeps track of the products in your fridge, allowing you to organise your shopping and cooking, easily and efficiently.     

 

The Details

Time to update your kitchen

Making shopping lists a thing of the past

Product Recognition

S.K.I.M will have the ability to recognise all forms of food products and dry goods that you use in your kitchen.

Inventory Synchronisation

S.K.I.M will then update the database with the items that have been identified, and place it in your inventory.

Image Capture

Placed in an open space, S.K.I.M will recognise the products and foods you place in its identification area, and match what it sees to products on the database.

Machine Learning

The more you use S.K.I.M, the more efficient and easy it will become. The system will learn the environment and prevent misidentification.

Compact Design

The design is aimed to be minimal and simple, as to not clutter or damage the flow of your kitchen. It will be mounted out of the way in a discreet location.

Customised Shopping Lists

As you use S.K.I.M, it will recognise the products you use most often. It will also pay attention to what your run out of. This will then allow S.K.I.M inventory to prepare custom shopping lists.
